Your customized Media School experience

Hands-on opportunities, expert faculty, overseas travel and more combine to prepare you for a lifetime of success in whichever media you choose.

International media travel opportunities

Report on HIV in Uganda. Make a film in Prague. Or intern in London. Because you won’t find the most compelling stories by sitting at your desk.

Top-notch facilities

Broadcast in the state-of-the-art Beckley Studio. Take in the news on our 24-foot high-definition screen. Experiment with virtual reality in the game lab. We’ve redefined the word “classroom.”

Industry-standard equipment

Canon 80D DSLR cameras. A Microsoft Hololens. Avid Composer workstations. Train on the tools you need to tell your story.

High-caliber faculty

Take a class with a Hollywood producer, a Pulitzer Prize-winning journalist, a former vice president of Saatchi & Saatchi or any of our internationally ranked researchers. Your mentors are experts in your field.

Unparalleled research opportunities

Work with faculty to explore human morality, news bias and more as part of a research team, and answer the questions that matter to you.

Real-world experience

Develop your own video game. Broadcast for the campus radio station. Intern for The New York Times. A Media School education only begins in the classroom.
